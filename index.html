The Green Sandles

Where are they gone the Green Sandles?
I was not made to be a mother,
I had too stricken a heart
So I wore green sandles to stay apart.

Then one night,
A man stood in an olive grove.
He beat viper till it bled
then drew me down low.
One night was all there was.
He fed me an olive leaf
and broke the strap off my dress.

I kept on the green sandles
but could not get off that hill.
Italy, where did you take me that night?
so dark and still.
He had an accent and a knife
and somewhere even had a wife.
He took my face and tore my hips
and planted in thier place something
new & strange, and near to love

I thought, I had nothing left
but you came later,
curved anew.

Forgive me.

I did not have means for catching
when I got poor Lucy.
